Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use Jowork, we may collect:

  • Account information (display name, email if provided)
  • Usage data (features accessed, session duration)
  • Data you connect through configured connectors

2. Self-Hosted Instances

If you run Jowork on your own infrastructure, all data stays on your machines. We have no access to your self-hosted data.

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and improve the Service
  • Authenticate your identity
  • Respond to support requests

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may share data only when required by law or with your explicit consent.

5. AI Model Interactions

When using hosted AI models, your prompts are sent to third-party AI providers (e.g., Anthropic, OpenAI). If you use your own API key (BYOK), interactions go directly to the provider under their terms.

6. Data Retention

You can delete your account and associated data at any time. Self-hosted data is entirely under your control.

7. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections and secure credential storage (AES-256-GCM).

8. Contact

For privacy concerns, contact us at support@jowork.work.
