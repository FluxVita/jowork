## Long Description (Product Hunt post body)

---

**Hey Product Hunt! 👋**

We're launching **Jowork** — an open-source, self-hosted AI coworker that actually knows your business.

Most AI tools are chat interfaces that answer questions in a vacuum. Jowork is different: it connects to your actual data sources — GitHub, Linear, Notion, Feishu, PostHog, Figma — and builds a **living context** about your product, team, and codebase.

Then it can **act**: run scheduled tasks, respond to events, execute goal-driven workflows, and communicate results back to you via chat, Telegram, or Slack.

**What makes it different:**

🔌 **Deep integrations** — 7+ connectors with more coming. Not just read-only; Jowork can create PRs, write Linear issues, send messages.

🤖 **Autonomous agent loop** — schedule recurring tasks ("summarize our sprint every Monday"), trigger on events ("when a P0 bug is filed, ping on-call"), or run goal-driven workflows.

🏠 **100% self-hosted** — your data never leaves your servers. Run on a Mac mini, a VPS, or Docker. No SaaS required.

🆓 **Free and open-source core** — AGPL-3.0, everything you need to self-host for your team. Premium features (sub-agents, event triggers) available for teams that want them.

🧠 **Three-layer context** — Company knowledge (docs, runbooks), team context (project state, conventions), and personal preferences — all assembled automatically for every query.

**Tech stack:** Node.js + TypeScript, Express 5, SQLite, Vue 3, Tauri (desktop), Bun sidecar.

We'd love to hear how you'd use an AI coworker that truly understands your business. Ask us anything!
