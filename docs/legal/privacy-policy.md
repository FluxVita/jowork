# Jowork Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 2026-03-05**

This Privacy Policy explains how FluxVita Inc. ("FluxVita", "we", "us", or "our") collects,
uses, and protects information when you use Jowork ("Service").

---

## 1. Who This Applies To

This policy applies to:

- **Self-hosted users** who download and run Jowork on their own infrastructure.
- **jowork.work account holders** who use our subscription and account services.

For self-hosted deployments, **FluxVita has no access to your data**. Your data stays on
your own servers.

## 2. Data We Collect

### 2.1 Self-Hosted Deployments

When you run Jowork on your own infrastructure, all of the following stays on your server:

- Conversations and messages
- Agent tasks and results
- Connected service credentials (stored encrypted in your local database)
- User accounts and roles
- Memory and context documents

FluxVita does **not** collect, store, or have access to any of this data.

### 2.2 jowork.work Account (Subscription Management)

If you create an account at `jowork.work` to manage a Premium subscription, we collect:

| Data | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Email address | Account identification, billing notifications |
| Billing information | Processed by Stripe; FluxVita stores only plan type and expiry |
| Jowork instance ID | To associate subscriptions with your deployment |
| Update check requests | Anonymous version string; no personal data |

We do **not** sell, rent, or trade this data with third parties.

### 2.3 Analytics (Optional)

If you opt in to anonymous usage analytics, we collect:

- Aggregate feature usage counts (e.g., "number of agents created")
- Performance metrics (e.g., "average request latency")
- Error rates (no stack traces or user data)

Analytics are opt-in and can be disabled at any time in your Jowork settings.

## 3. How We Use Data

We use the data described in Section 2 to:

1. Provide and improve the Jowork service.
2. Process subscription payments and manage your account.
3. Send critical security or service notices.
4. Comply with legal obligations.

## 4. Data Sharing

We share data only in the following limited circumstances:

- **Stripe** — to process payments. Stripe's [Privacy Policy](https://stripe.com/privacy) applies.
- **Legal requirements** — if required by law, court order, or government authority.
- **Business transfers** — if FluxVita is acquired, your data may transfer to the successor entity under the same privacy protections.

We do **not** share data with advertisers or data brokers.

## 5. Data Retention

- **Account data**: retained for the duration of your account plus 90 days after deletion.
- **Billing records**: retained for 7 years as required by tax law.
- **Anonymous analytics**: retained in aggregate form indefinitely.

## 6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+)
and at rest for all stored credentials. Subscription tokens are short-lived and rotated regularly.

For self-hosted deployments, security is your responsibility. We provide guidance in our
[security documentation](https://docs.jowork.work/security).

## 7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

- **Access** the personal data we hold about you.
- **Correct** inaccurate personal data.
- **Delete** your personal data (right to erasure).
- **Port** your data to another service.
- **Object** to certain processing.

To exercise these rights, email [privacy@jowork.work](mailto:privacy@jowork.work).

## 8. GDPR (EU Residents)

For EU residents, FluxVita processes personal data based on:

- **Contract performance** — to provide the subscription service you have purchased.
- **Legitimate interests** — for security monitoring and service improvement.
- **Legal obligations** — for tax and compliance purposes.

Our Data Protection Officer can be reached at [dpo@jowork.work](mailto:dpo@jowork.work).

## 9. CCPA (California Residents)

California residents have the right to know what personal information we collect, to
delete that information, and to opt out of its sale. We do not sell personal information.

To submit a request, email [privacy@jowork.work](mailto:privacy@jowork.work).

## 10. Children

Jowork is not directed at children under 13 (or 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly
collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal
data, contact us and we will delete it.

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We will notify you of material changes to this policy via email or via the Jowork changelog.
The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the latest revision.
