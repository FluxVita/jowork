# Jowork Refund Policy

**Last updated: 2026-03-05**

---

## Overview

We want you to be satisfied with Jowork Premium. This policy describes when and how
you can request a refund.

## 14-Day Money-Back Guarantee

If you are not satisfied with a **new** Pro, Team, or Business subscription, you may
request a full refund within **14 days** of the initial purchase.

To request a refund, email [billing@jowork.work](mailto:billing@jowork.work) with:

- Your account email address
- The date of purchase
- A brief description of why Jowork did not meet your needs (optional, but helps us improve)

Refunds are processed within 5–10 business days and returned to the original payment method.

## Annual Plan Refunds

For annual plans, the 14-day guarantee applies from the date of annual renewal or
initial purchase.

After the 14-day window, annual plans are **non-refundable** for the remainder of the
billing year. You may cancel to prevent future renewals, and the plan will remain
active until the end of the paid period.

## Monthly Plan Cancellations

Monthly plans can be cancelled at any time. No refund is issued for the current
billing month — access continues until the end of the period.

## Exceptions

Refunds will **not** be issued for:

- Accounts suspended or terminated for violation of our [Terms of Service](./terms-of-service.md).
- Requests submitted after the 14-day window (except at FluxVita's discretion).
- Cases where Jowork functionality was significantly impacted by your infrastructure,
  network, or third-party service failures.

## Downgrades

Downgrading from a higher-tier plan to a lower-tier plan (or Free) takes effect at the
end of the current billing period. Partial-period credits are not issued for downgrades.

## Contact

For billing questions or refund requests: [billing@jowork.work](mailto:billing@jowork.work)
